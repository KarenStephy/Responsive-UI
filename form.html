Validation rules used on this page

  • Title: no leading or trailing spaces.
  • Pages: numeric values with up to two decimals.
  • Date: `YYYY-MM-DD` format.
  • Tag: letters, spaces, and hyphens only.
  • Advanced regex: repeated words in titles are flagged automatically.
